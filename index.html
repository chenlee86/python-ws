Features

Everything a freelancer needs,
nothing they don't.

Built by a freelancer who was tired of spreadsheets, chasing invoices, and forgetting to log hours mid-meeting.

One-click time tracking

Start a timer from anywhere — browser extension, menu bar, or mobile. Assign to a client and project with a single tap.

Smart invoice generation

Select a time range, pick a client, and Meridian drafts a branded invoice with all the right line items. Review, adjust, send.

Live project reports

See budget burn, hours by client, and monthly revenue trends in real time. Export to PDF or CSV for client-facing reports.

Multi-device sync

Start a timer on your laptop and stop it on your phone. Everything syncs instantly so no hour slips through the cracks.

Client portal

Give each client a private link where they can view project progress, approved timesheets, and outstanding invoices.

Stripe & PayPal built-in

Accept payments directly on your invoices. Funds land in your account within two business days, no third-party fees beyond processor rates.

How it works

Up and running in under five minutes.

No implementation consultant. No onboarding call. Just sign up and start your first timer.

1

Create your workspace

Sign up with email or Google. Set your hourly rate and currency — Meridian handles the rest.

2

Add clients & projects

Import from a CSV or add manually. Each project has its own budget, rate, and status.

3

Log your hours

Use the timer or log time retroactively. Tag entries with tasks for detailed breakdowns.

4

Send & get paid

Generate an invoice in one click and send it from Meridian. Track when clients view it.
